## Reading Guide

This note is a compact algebra refresher for the structures that recur in geometry and physics: groups, rings, fields, vector spaces, modules, maps preserving structure, and the categorical habit of tracking objects through their morphisms.

Use it as a reference while reading the topology, differential-geometry, and bundle notes.
